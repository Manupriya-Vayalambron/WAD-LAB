Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, is an ivory-white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Completed in 1653, it is a symbol of eternal love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.
